# Growth Readiness Scorecard — Questions & Scoring

30 questions total, 5 per category, 4 answer options each (no plain yes/no — keeps it engaging and feels more like a real assessment). Each answer is worth 0, 1, 2, or 3 points.

**Scoring math:**
- Per question: 0–3 points
- Per category: 5 questions × 3 pts = 15 max → shown to user as a %
- Overall: 30 questions × 3 pts = 90 raw max → scaled to 0–100 (`round(raw / 90 * 100)`)

**Tiers (unchanged from earlier):**
- 0–39 Foundation Gap
- 40–69 Growth System Gap
- 70–84 Scale Ready
- 85–100 Performance Optimization

---

## 1. Offer Strength

1. **How would you describe your offer compared to competitors?**
   - It's basically the same as everyone else (0)
   - A little different, but hard to explain why (1)
   - Clearly different in 1–2 ways (2)
   - Clearly different and customers tell us so unprompted (3)

2. **Can you explain your offer in one sentence that a stranger would understand?**
   - No, it takes a few minutes to explain (0)
   - Sort of, but it sounds like a list of services (1)
   - Yes, with a little practice (2)
   - Yes, instantly and clearly (3)

3. **Do you give people a reason to act now rather than "someday"?**
   - No urgency or incentive at all (0)
   - We mention urgency occasionally (1)
   - We have a consistent reason to act now (2)
   - Yes, and it's a core part of the offer (3)

4. **How confident are you in your pricing relative to the value you deliver?**
   - Not confident — we guess or copy competitors (0)
   - Somewhat confident (1)
   - Confident, based on some testing (2)
   - Very confident, tested and validated (3)

5. **Have customers told you why they chose you over an alternative?**
   - Never asked (0)
   - Asked once or twice (1)
   - We ask sometimes (2)
   - We consistently track and use this feedback (3)

## Result page content per tier

For each tier, show:
1. Score (large, animated count-up)
2. Tier name + one-line diagnosis
3. The 2 lowest-scoring categories, named specifically (e.g. "Your biggest gap: Tracking & Measurement")
4. CTA: "Book a free Growth Accelerator Strategy Session"

Tier copy:

**0–39 — Foundation Gap**
"Your business isn't fully ready to scale with paid media yet. The good news: these are fixable, foundational issues — and fixing them first will make every dollar you spend later work harder."

**40–69 — Growth System Gap**
"You have some real pieces in place, but the system isn't strong enough yet to scale consistently. A few targeted fixes could meaningfully change your results."

**70–84 — Scale Ready**
"You're close. Your foundation is solid — the next gains come from campaign structure, creative testing, and optimization."

**85–100 — Performance Optimization**
"You already have a strong system. From here it's about deeper optimization to improve efficiency and scale further."
